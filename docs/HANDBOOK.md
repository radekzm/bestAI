# 📖 The Agentic Engineering Handbook (v1.0)

> How to lead, manage, and scale high-stakes software projects using Autonomous AI Swarms.

## 1. The Death of Prompt Engineering
Prompt engineering is advisory. Professionals don't "ask" AI to follow rules; they **enforce** them via deterministic governance (bestAI Hooks).

## 2. The Syndicate Methodology
The best results come from **Heterogeneous Swarms**.
- **The Lead (Claude):** Use for architecture, logic, and security.
- **The Scout (Gemini):** Use for mapping large codebases and research.
- **The Worker (Codex):** Use for repetitive boilerplate and UI.

## 3. Knowledge Convergence (Omni-GPS)
A team is only as strong as its shared memory. Human decisions (Nexus) and AI results (Vault) must converge in a single, locked Project Brain (`GPS.json`).

## 4. The 70/30 Rule
- **70% of AI effort** should be autonomous execution.
- **30% of Human effort** should be strategic check-ins (`bestai nexus`) and safety audits (`bestai compliance`).
